Travel Tips for Japan

Practical advice every beginner should know before traveling to Japan

Carry Cash

Many small shops, temples, and rural areas in Japan do not accept credit cards, especially outside major cities.

IMPORTANT TRAVEL TIP

Use Public Transportation

Trains and subways are reliable, clean, and punctual. Learn basic station signs and lines to move efficiently.

IMPORTANT TRAVEL TIP

Respect Local Etiquette

Politeness matters. Bow when greeting, avoid loud conversations, and follow local customs in public spaces.

IMPORTANT TRAVEL TIP

Carry Your Trash

Public trash bins are rare in Japan. Visitors are expected to carry their trash until they find proper disposal areas.

IMPORTANT TRAVEL TIP

Get Pocket Wi-Fi

Reliable internet helps with maps, translations, and transport. Pocket Wi-Fi rentals are common and easy to use.

IMPORTANT TRAVEL TIP

Learn Basic Phrases

Simple words like “Arigatou” and “Sumimasen” go a long way and are appreciated by locals.
